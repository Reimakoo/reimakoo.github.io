The Lost Vessel

The game is about a lone member of the coast guard who comes upon an abandoned ship in the middle of the ocean and sets out to investigate, only armed with a flashlight

The game was made in 8 weeks during the spring of 2024 and it was a collaboration between ICT students from Metropolia and graphic design students from Stadin Ammattiopisto
